Spring Mini-Jamboree

Date: Right after exams (see JobMarket-KeyDates calendar)

Right after exams, all day session for students and JMPO

  • 30 minute mini-job-talks for all students
  • All job candidates attend all talks

  • All advisors are expected to attend in person if at all possible

    • Will be available by Zoom for those unable to attend in person
  • Participation by other faculty who have worked with the student and know the student’s work is encouraged

  • All faculty are invited and encouraged to attend

    • Some of the most valuable feedback comes from faculty outside the student’s ﬁeld, who will ask the kinds of questions the student might get in a job market talk to the full faculty at another institution
  • Feedback for revising papers and presentations over the summer

Fall Jamboree

  • Share student preferences (job type; location; coordination problems)

    • 5 minutes at beginning should be devoted to these topics
  • Students learn from each other
  • All advisors are expected to attend in person if at all possible
  • Participation by other faculty who have worked with the student and know the student’s work is encouraged

  • All faculty are invited and encouraged to attend

    • Faculty learn about students (esp. those not in their ﬁelds)
    • Some of the most valuable feedback comes from faculty outside the student’s ﬁeld, who will ask the kinds of questions the student might get in a job market talk to the full faculty at another institution

  • Details - Students should:

    • Prepopulate their reservation for a time slot with their Zoom link
    • Resend memo to advisors and JMPO a couple of days before Jamboree
