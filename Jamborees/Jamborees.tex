\section*{Spring Mini-Jamboree}

Date: Right after exams (see \href{https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0?cid=amh1ZWNvbi5vcmdfYnUyM2xoNXA2a2IwbjMzYmprdWI0am5pa3NAZ3JvdXAuY2FsZW5kYXIuZ29vZ2xlLmNvbQ}{JobMarket-KeyDates} calendar)

Right after exams, all day session for students and JMPO
\begin{itemize}
  
\item 30 minute mini-job-talks for all students
\item All job candidates attend all talks
\item All advisors are expected to attend in person if at all possible
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Will be available by Zoom for those unable to attend in person
  \end{itemize}
\item Participation by other faculty who have worked with the student and know the student's work is encouraged
\item All faculty are invited and encouraged to attend
  \begin{itemize}
  \item Some of the most valuable feedback comes from faculty outside the student's field, who will ask the kinds of questions the student might get in a job market talk to the full faculty at another institution
  \end{itemize}
\item Feedback for revising papers and presentations over the summer
\end{itemize}

\section*{Fall Jamboree}

\begin{itemize}

  \end{itemize}
